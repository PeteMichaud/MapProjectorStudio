# Examples

## Options Usage

### Adjust

Adjusts the final output size in case your projection needs more room.

`--adjust -w 400 -h 400 --projection mercator -f ..\..\Tests\Input\earth_equirect.png -o ..\..\Tests\Output\Adjust.png`
